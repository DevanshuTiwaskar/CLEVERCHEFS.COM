Cooking Real Food

With the growing number of people making more sustainable choices with their food habits, take a look through our extensive list of vegan dishes that are absolutely delicious. We also understand getting peoples dietary requirements right may seem a little daunting, but the Executive Chef Team are highly skilled to be able to adapt our dishes to suit everyone. Not only are we able to cater to all sustainable choices, we also locally source all our meat & vegetables, and opt for ‘wonky veg’ where we can to help reduce the wastage of rejected foods.

We specialise in 4 sectors of the contract catering sector.Workplace with Métier. Independent Schools, Primary, Secondary Schools and Academies with Cleverchefs. And Event Catering and Hospitality with our Epic brands. Our passion for innovation in these sectors is the drive behind our success. Thinking differently is the key to our unique approach.
